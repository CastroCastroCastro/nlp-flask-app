Castro's NLP Project

What is NLP

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of Artificial Intelligence that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language. It enables computers to analyze, understand, and generate human language in meaningful ways.

Why Is It Important?

NLP is important because it helps computers communicate with humans in their own language and scales other language-related tasks. For example, NLP makes it possible for computers to read text, hear speech, interpret it, measure sentiment and determine which parts are important.

NLP Preprocessing Steps

The preprocessing steps in NLP are the initial steps that are performed on the text data before applying any NLP techniques. These steps include sentence segementation, tokenization, part-of-speech,stopword removal, lemmatization, and dependency parsing.

Project Overview

This project is a simple Flask web application that uses Wikipedia API to gather text from two Wikipedia pages, processes the text, and then compares the text to determine how similar the two pages are. The comparison is based on the semantic similarity of the content of the two pages.

How To Use The Application

To use the application, enter the titles of two Wikipedia pages in the input form below and click the "Compare" button. The application will fetch the content of the two pages, process the content, and then compare the content to determine the semantic similarity of the two pages. The processed tokens for each page will also be displayed.
